This worksheet will help you document and analyze the problems you encounter in your daily life. Use it to uncover potential business opportunities.

## Steps:
1. **List Problems**: Write down the problems you face regularly.
2. **Analyze Impact**: Assess how these problems affect you and others.
3. **Brainstorm Solutions**: Think of potential solutions to these problems.
