Before building their product, Dropbox’s founder, Drew Houston, created a demo video to explain how the service would work. This video went viral and validated the demand for Dropbox.

## Key Takeaways:
- **Test Before Building**: Dropbox validated their idea without writing a single line of code.
- **Leverage Simplicity**: A simple video was enough to communicate the value proposition.
- **Engage Early Adopters**: The video attracted early adopters who became loyal users.
