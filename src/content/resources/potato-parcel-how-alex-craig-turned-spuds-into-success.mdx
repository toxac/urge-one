Alex Craig came up with the idea of sending **messages written on potatoes** as a quirky gift. He launched **Potato Parcel**, allowing customers to send personalized messages on potatoes via mail.

## The Journey:
1. **Initial Idea**: Alex created a simple website to sell potato messages.
2. **Viral Success**: The idea went viral, and he made over **$100,000 in the first year**.
3. **Expansion**: He added new products, like sweet potatoes and customizable messages.
