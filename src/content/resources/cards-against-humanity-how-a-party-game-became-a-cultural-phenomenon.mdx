A group of friends created **Cards Against Humanity**, a card game described as "a party game for horrible people." They launched it on Kickstarter, and it became a **huge success**, selling millions of copies worldwide.

## The Journey:
1. **Initial Idea**: The game was created as a fun project among friends.
2. **Crowdfunding**: They raised over $15,000 on Kickstarter to fund production.
3. **Cultural Impact**: The game became a **cultural phenomenon**, with expansions and spin-offs.
