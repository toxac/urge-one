Sara Blakely was frustrated with how her pantyhose looked under white pants. She cut off the feet of her pantyhose to create a smoother silhouette and turned this simple solution into **Spanx**.

## The Journey:
1. **Initial Idea**: Sara created a prototype and patented her design.
2. **Bootstrapping**: She started the business with just $5,000 and no outside funding.
3. **Global Success**: Spanx became a **billion-dollar brand**, revolutionizing the shapewear industry.

## Key Takeaways:
- **Solve Real Problems**: Sara’s personal frustration led to a groundbreaking product.
- **Persistence Pays**: She faced countless rejections but never gave up.
- **Think Big**: From a simple idea to a global empire, Sara showed the power of vision and determination.
