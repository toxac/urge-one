Uber’s founders, Travis Kalanick and Garrett Camp, identified the problem of expensive and unreliable taxis during a trip to Paris. This observation led to the creation of a revolutionary ride-sharing platform.

## Key Takeaways:
- **Observe Pain Points**: Uber’s success started with identifying a real-world problem.
- **Leverage Technology**: The app-based model made ride-hailing more efficient.
- **Disrupt Traditional Models**: Uber challenged the taxi industry’s status quo.
