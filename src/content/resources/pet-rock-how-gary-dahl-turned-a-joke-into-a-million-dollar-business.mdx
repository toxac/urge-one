Gary Dahl came up with the idea of selling **Pet Rocks** as a joke. He packaged ordinary rocks in a box with a humorous manual on how to care for them.

## The Journey:
1. **Initial Idea**: Gary created a playful product that appealed to people’s sense of humor.
2. **Cultural Phenomenon**: The product became a **huge success**, selling over 1 million units in the 1970s.
3. **Legacy**: Pet Rocks remain a symbol of quirky, creative marketing.

## Key Takeaways:
- **Embrace Humor**: A funny, lighthearted idea can resonate with people.
- **Storytelling Matters**: The humorous manual made the product memorable.
- **Think Outside the Box**: Even the most ordinary objects can become extraordinary with the right approach.
