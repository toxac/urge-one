Alex Tew, a 21-year-old student, needed money to pay for his university tuition. He came up with the idea of **The Million Dollar Homepage**, a website with a **1,000 x 1,000 pixel grid** where each pixel could be purchased for $1. Advertisers could buy pixels and link them to their websites.

## The Journey:
1. **Initial Idea**: Alex launched the website with a simple concept: sell pixels to advertisers.
2. **Viral Success**: The idea went viral, and within months, all pixels were sold, earning Alex **$1 million**.
3. **Legacy**: The Million Dollar Homepage became a cultural phenomenon and inspired countless imitations.

## Key Takeaways:
- **Think Creatively**: Alex turned a simple idea into a million-dollar business.
- **Leverage Virality**: The internet’s power to spread ideas quickly can lead to massive success.
- **Start Small**: No initial investment was required—just a clever concept and execution.
